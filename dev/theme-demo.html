Dark Mode transition demo

Compare the candidate theme transitions on color swatches and sample 8-bit icons. Iris close-in is the new chosen effect: chunky blocks clear outer ring → inner ring, so the new theme floods in from the edges and the old theme shrinks to a block-circle at the center, with a glowing leading-edge ring that pulses inward (tune it with the sliders below). Pixel-dissolve is the previous effect (same blocks, random order). CRT power-cycle is the runner-up (collapse-to-line, then bloom open). Each replay button flips the theme; click again to flip back. Self-contained — no site assets required.
