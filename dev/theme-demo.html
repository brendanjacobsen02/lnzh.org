Compare the candidate theme transitions on color swatches and sample 8-bit icons. Iris close-in is the new chosen effect: small blocks clear from the edges inward along a noise-warped, tendrilled front — so the new theme corrodes its way in and the old theme is eaten down to a ragged dot at the center, a thin flickering edge closing like a parasite (tune it below; every flip warps differently). Pixel-dissolve is the previous effect (same blocks, random order). CRT power-cycle is the runner-up (collapse-to-line, then bloom open). Each replay button flips the theme; click again to flip back. Self-contained — no site assets required.
