Dark Mode transition demo

Compare the two candidate theme transitions on color swatches and sample 8-bit icons. Pixel-dissolve is the chosen effect (blocky ~24px checker redraw, ~500ms). CRT power-cycle is the runner-up (collapse-to-line, then bloom open). Each replay button flips the theme; click again to flip back. Self-contained — no site assets required.

Icons are CSS-grid pixel glyphs colored with var(--ink); in dark mode they re-tint to beige, mirroring the PNG -dark swap on the real site.
